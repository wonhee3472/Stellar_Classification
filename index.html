Important Features

Astronomical Filters

The u filter transmits ultraviolet light and the z filter transmits only infrared light. ultraviolet and infrared light are beyond the range of human vision. However, astronomers can still make images with these filters by colorizing the filtered images with colors we can see. The g, r, and i filters transmit visible light our eyes can see.
